Banana Split recipe

An exquisite, tasty banana based pudding for all dessert lovers. Extremely simple and quick to make since it requires no cooking, just add your toppings to a couple bananas and that's all!

Ingredients

  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream, or to taste
  • 2 almonds, chopped
  • 1 mochino cherry
  • 1 banana (or two small ones), sliced
  • 3 tablespoons whipped cream
  • any additional fruits you'd like

Directions

  1. Slice the banana or bananas and lay them together on a plate
  2. Put the whipped cream on top of the banana, distribute them in 3 similarly sized pats
  3. Chop up your almonds and sprinkle them evenly on top of the whipped cream
  4. Place the cherry on top of the highest whipped cream peak
