French Toast Recipe

This recipe is a treat! You will understand just how easy it is to make French toast, an excellent and relatively quick breakfast for those with a sweet tooth! Very customisable, you can use a multitude of different things to enhance your french toast, whatever you fancy.

Ingredients

  • 6 Thick White Bread Slices/Wholegrain if preferred
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • A Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Tablespon of Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
  4. Add syrup and/or any fruits you want
